2、sigmoid函数求导¶

sigmoid \begin{align} f'(x)&=(\frac{1}{1+e^{-x}})'\ &=\frac{e^{-x}}{(1+e^{-x})^2}\ &=\frac{1}{(1+e^{-x})}(1-\frac{1}{(1+e^{-x})})\ &=f(x)(1-f(x))\ \end{align}
